Project 2 was a news app that allows users to pick from a list of news sources to view. Once the user opens the app, they immediately get taken to the home page where they will given a list of news sources to choose from. Once clicking on the selected choice they get taken to a tableview filled with article choices. Upon clicking on a tableview Cell they get redirected to the actual article itself to read and enjoy its content. Some of the news sources the user will be able to choose from are popular new sources such as Fox Sports, CNN, IGN News and much more.
